## Notes

- The Santiago-Visser (2022) rebuttal of Lentz (2021) is significant: it showed that checking energy conditions only for co-moving observers is insufficient.
- Subluminal positive-energy warp drives (Bobrick & Martire 2021) are valid but offer no advantage over conventional propulsion.
- The ER=EPR conjecture remains unproven and provides no pathway to macroscopic traversable wormholes.
