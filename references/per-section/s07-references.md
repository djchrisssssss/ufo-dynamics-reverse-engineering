## Key Synthesis Points

| Domain | Theory Status | Materials Status | Gap |
|--------|--------------|-----------------|-----|
| Spacetime distortion | Valid GR solution | No fabrication method | 10²⁶× precision |
| Exotic matter | Casimir proves concept | Microscopic only | 10⁶⁸× energy |
| Fusion energy | Well-understood | Not compact enough | 10¹–10²× scale |
| Structural materials | CNT theory validated | Bulk performance gap | ~30× strength |
| Metamaterial cloaking | Microwave demonstrated | Optical losses | Qualitative |
| Energy-to-curvature | No theory exists | No mechanism | Undefined |

## Notes

- Central thesis: barriers are primarily materials science maturity, not fundamental physics violations.
- Exception: the energy-to-curvature transduction gap is a missing-physics problem, not a materials problem.
- Historical precedent (semiconductor theory → transistor: ~15 years; superconductivity → HTS: ~75 years) suggests materials gaps eventually close, but timescales are unpredictable.
